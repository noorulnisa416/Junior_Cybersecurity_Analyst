## 📋 Course Overview

This course introduces the field of cybersecurity:

- What the cybersecurity field is and why it matters
- Why it is considered a **future-proof career**
- How personal and organizational data is protected from attacks
- What techniques cybercriminals use
- What a career in cybersecurity looks like

## 1️⃣ Module 1: Introduction to Cybersecurity
🔗 [Module Link](https://www.netacad.com/launch?id=fb5c52c1-1f98-4900-aea4-663f26884b4d&tab=learning&assignment=0505f308-315c-4980-90e6-113d913c41c6&activeTab=assignmentdetails) | 📝 [Module 1 Exam](https://www.netacad.com/launch?id=fb5c52c1-1f98-4900-aea4-663f26884b4d&tab=learning&assignment=8c56ccca-080a-4f7e-b864-633c2105c887&activeTab=assignmentdetails)

- **Define:** Covers the basic definition of cybersecurity — data breaches, types of hackers, and the value of data in the digital world.
- **Why should I do this?** Without understanding the basics, later concepts become difficult to follow — this is the foundation module.
- **What benefit will I get?** A clear understanding of why cyberattacks matter and how they affect people and organizations daily.

---

## 2️⃣ Module 2: Attacks, Concepts and Techniques
🔗 [Module Link](https://www.netacad.com/launch?id=fb5c52c1-1f98-4900-aea4-663f26884b4d&tab=learning&assignment=1897f5eb-7042-4566-ab21-b048a1ca6ce6&activeTab=assignmentdetails) | 📝 [Module 2 Exam](https://www.netacad.com/launch?id=fb5c52c1-1f98-4900-aea4-663f26884b4d&tab=learning&assignment=7d0f7db7-9824-4e87-919c-cc4efcba318c&activeTab=assignmentdetails)

- **Define:** Explains different attack types — malware, phishing, social engineering, DoS attacks — and the methods attackers use.
- **Why should I do this?** Without understanding how an attacker thinks, building a proper defense is difficult.
- **What benefit will I get?** The ability to recognize real-world attack patterns — a core skill for any security analyst.

---

## 3️⃣ Module 3: Protecting Your Data and Privacy
🔗 [Module Link](https://www.netacad.com/launch?id=fb5c52c1-1f98-4900-aea4-663f26884b4d&tab=learning&assignment=933a6d54-facf-4cfa-a2a4-00b79c36dd15&activeTab=assignmentdetails) | 📝 [Module 3 Exam](https://www.netacad.com/launch?id=fb5c52c1-1f98-4900-aea4-663f26884b4d&tab=learning&assignment=3d3acb9a-f7ff-414c-b417-324523396157&activeTab=assignmentdetails)

- **Define:** Covers protecting data and privacy at a personal level — strong passwords, safe browsing, and protecting personal information on social media.
- **Why should I do this?** These are practical, everyday skills that anyone can use, regardless of career path.
- **What benefit will I get?** Improved personal digital safety, and an understanding of why protecting end-users is such a large part of security.

---

## 4️⃣ Module 4: Protecting the Organization
🔗 [Module Link](https://www.netacad.com/launch?id=fb5c52c1-1f98-4900-aea4-663f26884b4d&tab=learning&assignment=0e5cfbba-09a9-41c4-9aa7-25020e6eb8a8&activeTab=assignmentdetails) | 📝 [Module 4 Exam](https://www.netacad.com/launch?id=fb5c52c1-1f98-4900-aea4-663f26884b4d&tab=learning&assignment=4aac2401-a136-4296-880a-4969f3a09bea&activeTab=assignmentdetails)

- **Define:** Explains the technologies, processes, and procedures organizations use to protect their systems and data — firewalls, security policies, incident response.
- **Why should I do this?** In a real job, you'll be responsible for protecting an entire organization, not just yourself.
- **What benefit will I get?** An entry-level understanding of how a company structures and manages its security.

## 5️⃣ Module 5: Will Your Future Be in Cybersecurity?
🔗 [Module Link](https://www.netacad.com/launch?id=fb5c52c1-1f98-4900-aea4-663f26884b4d&tab=learning&assignment=cc10bc18-a50a-4998-af22-3b8266c0deb0&activeTab=assignmentdetails) | 📝 [Module 5 Exam](https://www.netacad.com/launch?id=fb5c52c1-1f98-4900-aea4-663f26884b4d&tab=learning&assignment=7d298ee5-cedd-4e7a-b4f8-9cbb293ec45b&activeTab=assignmentdetails)

- **Define:** Discusses career paths in cybersecurity, roles (SOC analyst, penetration tester, etc.), and relevant certifications.
- **Why should I do this?** Understanding the available options in the field is essential before making a career decision.
- **What benefit will I get?** Clarity on which direction to pursue next and which certification is the right fit.
